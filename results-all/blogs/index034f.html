                                 Yvette Baker Trophy 2014

A small but select band of Lakeland juniors made the trip down Frodsham in Cheshire on Sunday to compete in the regional round of the Yvette Baker Trophy competition.

Within region SROC & SELOC were unable to field full teams, Border Liners chose to attend the NEOA round, and MDOC & WCOC didn't attend. So we found ourselves in a 3 way match with PFO & DEEside, who were hosting the round.

Despite class wins from Heather Ball, Hannah Cleary-Hughes, Alex Foster and Will Rigg the juniors were narrowly beaten into second place by DEE, with PFO third.
