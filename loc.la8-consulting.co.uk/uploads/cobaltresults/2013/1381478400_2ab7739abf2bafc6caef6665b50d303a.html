LOC School Orienteering Series 2010       SERPENTINE WOODS      4th May 2010

 

Apologies once again for the electronics letting us down on the day.  We have now calculated the times from the result slips and verified the controls visited. Where a competitor has missed (m) one control or punched just one wrong (w) control we have indicated this but still shown a time in brackets.  Where there are multiple omissions and wrong controls we have not shown a time.

We were very impressed by the number of children who successfully completed courses although there are still some who are punching everything they pass without checking the codes.
