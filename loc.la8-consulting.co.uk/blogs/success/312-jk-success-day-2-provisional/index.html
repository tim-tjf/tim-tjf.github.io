Two Golds and many other medals at JK 2011

Details
Last Updated on 02 November 2012

Bob Stewardson dominated M80, winning both days and taking the overall title by over 40 minutes.

In the W165+ Relay, Judy Burge, Carol McNeill,Helena Burrows won an excellent Gold, coming in less than a minute in front of Happy Herts.

Natalie Beadle surpassed herself on Day 2 of the JK and lead W16A by over 2 minutes.  Her time of 42:50 put her well ahead of a very strong W16 field.  Day 3 saw her run a good race, but she placed 4th with her time of 41:55 leaving her 15 seconds off the overall winner in Silver.

Katie Wright, a double JK Champion last year, had an excellent run on Day 3 to place 2nd on the same class.  Her time of 40:23 puller her up to 5th overall, from 12th on Day 2. 

New recruit, Helena Burrows, won Day 2 on W60L, but could only manage 8th place on Day 3, dropping back to 3rd overall.

Carol McNeill came an uncharacteristic 3rd on Day 2, pulling back from 27th after a disastrous second leg.  Her Day 3 was better, coming 2nd and pulling her up to 2nd place overall.

Another Silver went to the W48- team of Carrie Beadle,Hannah Cleary-Hughes and Natalie Beadle.  They missed out on gold by just 3 seconds.

In M65L Tony Richardson won Bronze, pulling up from 5th on Day 2 with a 3rd place on Day 3.

Carl Edmonds was just outside the medals in 4th place on the highly competitive M18E class.
