Dear All,

It made a pleasant change for me to sit in the warmth and comfort of the cafe instead of the car and organise the events. I hope you all enjoyed your run around Staveley even if parts were a bit muddy, sorry Jonty I hope your shoes clean up.

One control, No. 47, was reported as missing by a number of early runners although later runners did find the sign lying on the ground upside down. In this case we gave points to all runners who told us they visted this control.

This was the last event in this years league and shortly after the last runners finished we were able to award prizes to the class winners in each category due to the kind support of our sponsors Wilfs Cafe and Pride Health and Fitness in Kendal. Charlotte and Jane did an excellent job in catering for all the runners who had built up an appetite running around the streets of Staveley, many thanks for your support and hard work.

Congratulations to the overall class winners for 2002

Juniors            Thomas and Laura Chaldecott
Biker               Dave Downes
Male                Phil Clark
Female           Janet Nash
Pairs               Diane and Nigel Walker 

Many thanks for your support

George and Myra
