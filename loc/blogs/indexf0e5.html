Last Updated on 22 January 2014

LOC/WAROC Night Event Series 2013

 

Sponsored by Haglofs

 

Congratulations to the Winners of the LOC/WAROC Night Event Series 2013, in which 75 people took part.

The Winners are:

M16+           Will Rigg                                  LOC

M21+           Bruce Duncan                           LOC

M50+           Richard Lecky-Thompson          LOC

 

W16+         Katie Wright                               LOC

W21+         Jo Cleary                                    LOC

W50+         Rebekah Beadle                          LOC

 

Prizes, donated by Haglofs, will be presented at the LOC Club Chmpionships on 12th January 2014.

This series has been very successful. Despite dreadful weather on some occasions, turnout has been very good, encouraging us to organise more events of this type during the winter and autumn months next year.

Many thanks for your support.

 

Dick Towler

Member Success 2014

Details
Last Updated on 18 March 2014

                                       Member Success 2014

 

Lakeland have a British Night Champion with Dick Towler in M65. Mole Valley club hosted on Saturday February 22nd at Pippingford Park, results at www.mvoc.org. Diana McClure also made the podium with 3rd in W65.

Beryl Offley was 6th in W65, Derek Fryer 10th in M60 and John Pullin 10th in M65.

 

Whilst we have been enjoying the rain, Hollie Orr and Will Rigg escaped to Spain to compete in the Andalucia O-Meet. Both produced top 10 results in an international field in D21E & H18A respectively.

Winsplits results for Sprint/Middle/Long races can be found via the front page of the event website at www.aom2014.com.
