Level 2/3 Coaching Spring 2013

Last Updated on 22 January 2014

Our coaching for Levels 2/3 (Yellow and Orange standard) is starting again this
weekend.

Unfortunately we cannot staff this coaching for all of the Saturday morning
coaching events.

Feb 2nd at Tarn Hows

Time:- 10am -12 noon
Max Numbers of participants:- 10/12
Minimum knowledge / experience:- you must have attended at least one
event.
Cost £2.00 adults, £1.00 under 18
Coaching level 2-3 (Yellow and orange)
Age limits :- Primary yrs 5/6 … adult. Any primary school child must be
