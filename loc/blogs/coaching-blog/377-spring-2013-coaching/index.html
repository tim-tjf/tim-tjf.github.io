Spring 2013 Coaching

Details
Last Updated on 22 January 2014

This Spring the Saturday coaching starts on Saturday 2nd Febuary.

It is open to level 2 and 3 (Yellow and Orange) as well as level 4 and 5 (Light Green to Brown). [To be confirmed]

Please email the planner/organiser if you intend to come - then you will get a map! And will be told if there are any changes.

They are usually 10am to 12noon and cost £2 seniors £1 juniors, but please see the individual notices for any changes.

Any juniors under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

Please come with suitable warm clothing, a snack and drink to rehydrate.
