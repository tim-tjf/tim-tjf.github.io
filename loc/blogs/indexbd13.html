LOC Achieve podium position at CompassSport Cup Final

Details
Last Updated on 22 January 2014

COMPASS SPORT CUP FINAL - OCTOBER 20th

Lakeland acheived 3rd place at Sunday's CompassSport Cup Final near Bristol.

This fantastic result was despite losing around 15 top athletes to a fixtures clash with the FRA Relays, illness and injuries. The event was held in the Forest of Dean and hosted by Bristol Orienteering Klubb who were victorious on home ground. 

The most impressive result was achieved by Daisy Fletcher-Cooney who won the Orange Girls category.

Full results can be found on the Bristol website http://www.bristolorienteering.org.uk/results

LOC/WAROC Night Event Series 2013

This year, we are hosting a weekly series of 7 night events, on Wednesday evenings, starting on 6th November in Bowness. 4 of these events will follow the mainly urban WARLOC format, established in recent years, with a mass start on a single course. The other 3 events will be 1 hour Score events in typical orienteering terrain.

To make the series more exciting, there will be a competition for both sexes in 3 age class categories, Junior M/W16+, Senior M/W21+ and Veteran M/W50+, with each runner’s best 4 results to count. Points may mean prizes!!

The WARLOC events will have a mass start at 18:30; the Night Score events will have individual start times between 1800 and 1900. Entry fees will be £4/adult, £2/junior. Juniors under 16 must be accompanied by an adult at WARLOC events.
