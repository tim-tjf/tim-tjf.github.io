                         LOC/WAROC Night Event Series 2013                                

                                       Sponsored by Haglofs  

The Ambleside event was run from Bilbos Cafe on Wednesday December 4th and incorporated a fast traverse of the north end of the town followed by a loop through a steep wooded hillside. Best points for artistic merit went to Nick Howlett for his forward roll onto one of the paths followed by a swift gathering of various pieces of his head torch by other competitors.

Results after 5 of the seven events can be found here files/WarLoc_Series_2013_afterAmbleside.pdf .
