Medals at the British Sprint and Middle Distance 2011

Details
Last Updated on 02 November 2012

A few hardy souls travelled South to Worth Forest for the 2011 British Sprint and Middle Distance Champs.

Thomas Edmonds won Bronze on the M12 Sprint at Brighton.

Adam Bartlett and Diane McClure were the pick of the LOC crop at the Middle Distance with 2nd in M14 and 3rd in W60 respectively.

Other creditable performances came from Carl Edmonds 4th M18, Natalie Beadle 4th W16 and Beryl Offley and Elizabeth Hindle 5th and 6th W55.

Well done to them all.

LOC members represent England at Interland

Details
Last Updated on 02 November 2012

Lois Jefferson (W14) and Natalie Beadle (W16) both represented England in the recent Interland competition in Germany.  Adam Bartett and Carl Edmonds were both reserves.

Lois came 2nd and Natalie 4th (first 4 in 38 seconds) - congratulations to both of them. Adam had a top time on an open course.

England won the Team Competition!
