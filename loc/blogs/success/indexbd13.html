Medals at World Schools Champs in Italy

Details
Last Updated on 02 November 2012

Adam Bartlet won an individual Silver in the Middle Distance race at the World Schools Championships in Italy.

The UVHS Junior Boys team had a storming second day to move from 5th to win the Bronze Team Medal in the Schools race.

The Junior Girls Select team, including LOC's Natalie Beadle, also took Bronze.

BOC Medals

Details
Last Updated on 02 November 2012

LOC had two good medals in the major classes at the Individual British Champs near Sheffield.  Lois Jefferson won a great Silver Medal in the W14A and Carol McNeill won Bronze in W65L.

In the Relays, Gold went to the M14 team of Andrew Brockbank, James Allison and Adam Bartlett.  Gold also went to the W60+ team of Carol McNeill, Helena Burrows and Diana McClure, who led all the way.
